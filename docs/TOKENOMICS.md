## Token Utility

### Platform
- **Task rewards** — creators can pay in NXM or SOL
- **Staking** — stake NXM to become dispute resolver, earn fees
- **Governance** — 1 NXM = 1 vote on protocol upgrades

### Fee Discount
- Hold 1,000 NXM → 1.5% fee (vs 2.5% standard)
- Hold 10,000 NXM → 1.0% fee
- Hold 100,000 NXM → 0.5% fee

### SBT Boost
- Stake NXM alongside tasks to accelerate reputation gain
- 1,000 NXM staked = +2x reputation per completed task

## Dispute Resolution Staking

To become a dispute resolver:
1. Stake minimum 10,000 NXM
2. Participate in dispute votes
3. Earn 50% of platform fees from resolved disputes
4. Slashed if vote with minority (bad faith protection)
